About Us

Located in the city of Gothenburg, we celebrate pancakes every day. With organic ingredients only, and close collaborations with local farmers we bake our delicious pancakes from scratch. We then top it with fruit and berries that are in season. Try our Pancake of the week that our chef combined for a tasty experience!

Our mission is to spread smiles and joy by serving our crave-worthy pancakes. So gather your friends and come in and try them yourself!
